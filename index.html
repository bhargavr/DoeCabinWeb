Welcome to Doe Cabin

Let the journey begin !!

Hidden jewel of sierra nevada valley

Yes, this lovely cottage – Doe Cabin nestled on top of the Sierra Nevada mountains offers excitement for all. Aptly named “Doe Cabin”, Does and Bucks are permanent guests at the greenery around the cabin. Located far away from the busy world, Doe Cabin is a haven for the ones looking for a place settled cozily in nature’s lap.

There's nothing better on a rainy day than to soak in a bubble bath

After all the trekking and hiking, nothing is more warm and welcoming than the cozily furnished Doe Cabin. The cottage has cozy bedrooms. The game room has some amusing games, if you choose to stay indoors and enjoy a relaxed holiday with your loved ones.

This kitchen is seasoned with Love

A modern and fully stocked kitchen with all the modern amenities to cook your favourite meal. The patio offers serene views of the Sierra Nevada. After a tired day, have a moonlight dinner on the patio gazing at the stars, just listening to nature. What can be more romantic than a moonlight dinner with stars twinkling on a clear sky.
